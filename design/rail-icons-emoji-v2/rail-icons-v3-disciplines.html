BuildMyMTB · Discipline Icons — Round 3 (big sweep)

Streamlined per your call: the five rider-discipline chips only, with a ton of fresh samples. Nothing from rounds 1–2 is re-offered — every glyph here is new to the board, and ids continue each slot's numbering (you can still reference any old id).

How to reply

  • Per-slot picks: "DA-21, DH-6, DE-13, DT-10, DX-6."
  • A quintet: "Q1" — the last page shows five curated five-icon rails, coherent across the rail.
  • Mix freely, including ids from rounds 1–2 if one grew on you.

Wildcard rows: each slot ends with a dashed, grey-tagged row of ~5 monochrome/dingbat marks that bend the emoji style — included because two emoji rounds missed; ignore them freely.

What each slot is telegraphing (the bar at 20 px)

  • All — the union: everything, no filter.
  • DH — steep, descent, gravity, full-face.
  • Enduro — big mountains, timed stages, long days.
  • Trail — forest, flow, singletrack.
  • XC — speed, endurance, lightweight.
